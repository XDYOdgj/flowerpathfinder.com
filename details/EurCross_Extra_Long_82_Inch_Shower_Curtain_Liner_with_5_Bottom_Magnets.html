EurCross Extra Long Shower Curtain Liner

This high-quality shower curtain liner is the perfect solution for anyone looking to enhance their bathroom experience. With an impressive 82-inch length and 72-inch width, it perfectly fits larger showers or bathtubs, offering more space and a better fit than standard options.
